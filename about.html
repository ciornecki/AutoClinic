About Us

Learn More About Our Company and Our Mission

Our Mission

At AutoClinic, our mission is to provide our customers with exceptional auto repair and maintenance services. We believe that every customer deserves honesty, transparency, and respect, and we strive to deliver those values in everything we do. Our team of experienced mechanics and staff are committed to delivering high-quality workmanship and personalized service, ensuring that every vehicle leaves our shop in top condition. We are dedicated to staying up-to-date with the latest industry standards and technology, and we continuously invest in our staff and equipment to provide the best service possible. At AutoClinic, we are passionate about cars and our customers, and we work tirelessly to exceed their expectations.

Our Story

AutoClinic was founded in 2010 by two lifelong friends, John and Michael, who shared a passion for cars and a desire to provide exceptional service to their customers. They started out in a small garage, working long hours to repair and maintain vehicles for friends and family. As their reputation for quality workmanship and personalized service grew, so did their business. Today, AutoClinic is a thriving auto repair service with a team of experienced mechanics and staff who are committed to delivering the same level of service that John and Michael started with a decade ago.

At AutoClinic, we take pride in our roots and remain dedicated to our mission of providing exceptional service to every customer who walks through our doors. Our focus on honesty, transparency, and respect for our customers sets us apart from other auto repair services. We believe that communication is key to building trust and delivering the best possible service, and we take the time to explain every repair and maintenance service to our customers. Our team is passionate about cars and dedicated to staying up-to-date with the latest industry standards and technology, so that we can continue to deliver the best possible service to our customers.
